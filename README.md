## 🌌 Why This Exists

Every morning, millions of researchers, engineers, and curious minds open ten different tabs — arXiv, YouTube, Hacker News, random blogs — hoping something relevant surfaces. Most of it doesn't. Hours pass. Nothing useful was found. The algorithm gave you what it wanted you to see, not what you needed.

**For You was built to flip that.**

It's a tiny app that lives in your Mac's menu bar — no Dock icon, no notifications screaming at you, no engagement traps. Just a quiet ✦ sparkle waiting for when *you* decide it's time to learn.

---

## 🔥 The Problem It Solves

> *"I spend more time looking for good content than actually reading it."*

Sound familiar? Here's what's broken about how we discover research today:

| The Old Way | The For You Way |
|---|---|
| 🕳️ Algorithmic rabbit holes | 🎯 You define the topics |
| 🗞️ Same 5 popular posts everyone sees | 🔬 Latest papers + fresh blogs + new talks |
| 💀 Clickbait, memes, listicles everywhere | 🧹 AI filters out the noise before you see it |
| ⏱️ Hours wasted scrolling | ⚡ Syncs in the background, ready when you are |
| 🧩 10 different apps and tabs | ✦ One quiet icon in your menu bar |

## 💡 What It Does

You tell it what you care about. That's it.

Type in topics like `"Reinforcement Learning"`, `"Robotics"`, `"Quantum Computing"` — whatever your brain is obsessed with right now. Then forget about it.

Every few hours, **For You** goes out into the internet and brings back:

- 📄 **The freshest research papers** — straight from arXiv, the world's biggest open science archive
- 🌐 **Real blog posts** from engineers and researchers actually doing the work — not "Top 10 AI tools you need!!!" garbage
- 🎥 **YouTube talks and lectures** — conference keynotes, deep dives, seminars

Then it reads every single one. It writes you a summary. It pulls out the key ideas. It scores how important each piece is. All before you even open the app.

When you click that ✦ icon, your feed is waiting. Clean. Curated. Yours.

---

## 🧠 The Intelligence Behind It

For You uses **Google's Gemini AI** as its brain. Not just to summarize — to *think*.

It asks questions like:
- *Is this actually new research, or just someone repackaging old ideas?*
- *Is this a listicle designed to waste time, or genuine technical depth?*
- *How credible are the authors? What's the citation weight behind this?*
- *On a scale of 0–10, how much does this actually matter?*

Anything that doesn't pass the bar gets **silently discarded**. You never see it. It never existed.

What's left is pure signal.

## 💬 Go Deeper With Chat

Found something interesting? Tap the chat button on any item and ask it anything.

> *"Explain the key contribution like I'm a grad student in a different field"*

> *"What are the practical applications of this in robotics?"*

> *"How does this compare to the previous state of the art?"*

The AI has read the full paper or article. It remembers your previous questions in that thread. It answers like a knowledgeable colleague, not a search engine.

## 🎯 The Philosophy

**For You is built on one belief:** your attention deserves respect.

Not to be harvested. Not to be monetized. Not to be stretched thin across forty browser tabs.

This app will **never**:
- 🚫 Recommend content to keep you scrolling longer
- 🚫 Show you trending posts just because they're trending  
- 🚫 Surface clickbait, memes, or filler content
- 🚫 Add a social feed, comments, or engagement metrics
- 🚫 Send you push notifications designed to pull you back in

Every decision in this app — every design choice, every filter, every feature — is made by asking one question:

*Does this give the user more signal, or does it add noise?*

If it adds noise, it doesn't ship.

## ⬇️ Download

**No setup. No accounts. No subscriptions.**

1. Go to the **[Releases page](../../releases/latest)**
2. Download `ForYou-x.x.x.zip`
3. Unzip → drag `ForYou.app` to your `/Applications` folder
4. Right-click → Open (first launch only, macOS security prompt)
5. The ✦ icon appears in your menu bar

You'll need a free **[Google Gemini API key](https://aistudio.google.com/apikey)** — takes 2 minutes to get, completely free tier available. Paste it in Settings when the app asks.

---

## 🛡️ Privacy

Everything runs **on your Mac** and **your API keys**. There is no For You server. There is no analytics. There is no telemetry. The app can't even phone home — it doesn't have an address to call.

Your research interests are yours.
